About APL

The story of passion, cricket, and community in Gobardanga

Our Story

Ambagan Premier League is played in Gobardanga, a small town in North 24 Parganas, West Bengal. Specifically located at Babupara in Debraj Mukharjee's Garden, where the league is played every day.

APL was founded by Alamgir Mondal in 2017. Since then, many teams like Ambagan Knight Riders and Babupara Super Kings have participated in this historic league.
